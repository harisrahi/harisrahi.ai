Beat 09 · The Bet

SFDC's bet.

Customers build on the moat, not around it.

Scenario A

They may be right.

The moat compounds. Agents inside become the obvious place to build. Customers consolidate.

Scenario B

They may be partially right.

Some workloads stay on-platform; others slip out. SFDC keeps the deep ones, loses the long tail.

Scenario C

The moat may evaporate.

Faster than anyone expected, including SFDC. Agents access the data, then route around the workflows.

What's not in dispute: they see the shift, and they're moving on it.
