The Insight

Most AI adoption programs get the sequencing wrong.

People complete a training. Return to their desks. Within weeks, revert to exactly how they worked before. It's not motivation. It's not talent. It's context.

AI fluency only develops when the learning and the doing are the same activity. Separate them, and the training evaporates.

The platform changes. This principle persists.
