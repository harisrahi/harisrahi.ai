The Takeaway

What AI gives back depends on what you bring to it.

01 You have to think critically and resist letting AI's confidence replace yours.
02 Be precise with what you put in. AI knows nothing about you, your work, or your intent unless you say it.
03 Verify everything it gives back. Apply your discernment, your judgment, and your instinct before you use it.
