Privacy Notice

This site is built on a simple principle: collect nothing beyond what's needed to operate it. In a market where data is harvested by default, that's a deliberate choice.

Notice Details

Plain language privacy details.

This notice covers site tracking, server-side analytics, email contact, and the boundary between this site and client engagement data.

Tracking

There are no cookies, no tracking pixels, no advertising, no social media embeds, and no third-party trackers of any kind.

Analytics and logs

Analytics are aggregate and server-side only, reporting pages viewed, referral sources, and visitor countries without identifying anyone. This site's hosting provider keeps standard server logs, including IP addresses, for security and reliability, and retains them for a maximum of 30-days.

Email contact

If you contact me by email, I receive your address and whatever you choose to include. I use it only to respond and to conduct any business that follows. I don't sell personal information, share it for marketing, or keep it longer than needed.

Client engagements

Data handled inside client engagements is governed by contract and confidentiality terms, not by this notice.
